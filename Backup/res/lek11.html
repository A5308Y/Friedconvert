#The sentence structure#

#In simple sentences the rule for the sequence of words is: the finite (conjugated) verb stands in the 2nd place in the sentence. Other parts of the sentence can change their positions.

The subject (the active element) (in our example: Susi) stands in the 1st position, but can also change position with another part of the sentence.


1st position 2nd position
